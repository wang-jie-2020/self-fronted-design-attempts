Questions teams usually ask before moving their knowledge base.

How long does migration take? +

Most teams import existing docs, playbooks, and notes in under one day. You can move in phases by team or by workspace.

Can we control who edits critical pages? +

Yes. You can set page-level permissions, reviewer requirements, and workspace roles for write access and approvals.

Does search include old revisions? +

Search focuses on current content by default, and lets you include historical versions when you need audit context.

How do shortcuts work in the editor? +

Common actions are keyboard-first, including quick search ⌘K and page switch ⌘P.
